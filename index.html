Akira SuperStorm

High Quality

If you are looking for a quality guitar at an affordable price, the SuperStorm is a perfect option. Its knob wood fingerboard, along with its ergonomic shape, will allow you to play comfortably, reducing fatigue. The Canadian maple neck will offer you quality, resistance and elegance. Finally, the baked poplar body wood will give this electric guitar a clean and impeccable sound.
